This is a pizza box.

It can only be recycled if it is not soiled.

If your pizza box looks like this inside then it cannot be recycled:

This box is one of the rare unicorns that can be recycled:

Your pizza box may look like this if your pizza was not very greasy AND it had wax paper inside that soaked up all of the grease. This is highly uncommon.

Many boxes have tops that are clean enough to be recycled.
Just tear off the top, recycle it and throw the greasy bottom part away.

**You can even compost the greasy parts it if that option is available to you**
