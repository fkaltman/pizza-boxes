A pizza box can only be recycled if it is not soiled.

This box is one of the rare unicorns
that can be recycled:

Your pizza box may look like this if your pizza was not very greasy
AND it had wax paper inside that soaked up all of the grease. This is highly uncommon.

I know you are keen on recycling, or you wouldn't be checking out this page.
But don't be sad, on the up side most boxes have tops that are clean enough to be recycled. Just tear off the clean top, recycle that part and throw the greasy bottom part away. You can even compost the greasy parts it if that option is available to you.
